Support & Feedback

How can we help?
At MyMoments, we believe the anticipation of life’s greatest milestones should be as beautiful and stress-free as possible. If you’re having trouble with the app or have suggestions for new features, we’re here to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a new countdown? Simply tap the "+" button at the bottom of the Home screen. Give your moment a name, select the date, and choose one of our exclusive tactile backgrounds to match the vibe.
2. Can I use my own photos? Yes! When creating or editing a moment, tap "Choose from Photos" to personalize your countdown with your own memories.
3. How do I enable notifications? Go to Settings within the app. Here you can toggle notifications on/off and set a specific time for your daily reminder, so you can enjoy the countdown every day.
